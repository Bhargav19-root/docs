## Welcome to linkutm Documentation

Your complete guide for managing, tracking, and analyzing campaign links with UTM parameters. linkutm is a comprehensive platform designed for marketers, agencies, and teams who need powerful link management and analytics.

## What is linkutm?

linkutm is a comprehensive UTM link management and tracking platform that combines link creation, shortening, analytics, and team collaboration into one powerful solution. Whether you're running social media campaigns, email marketing, or multi-channel promotions, linkutm helps you track performance and make data-driven decisions.
