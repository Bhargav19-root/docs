## What linkutm does

Three things, end to end:

1. **Build** - create campaign URLs with consistent UTMs (templates + rules).
2. **Shorten** - wrap them under a short code, optionally on your branded domain.
3. **Measure** - every click captured with geo, device, referrer, and source.

<Note>
Already running ads? Skip to [Templates](/utm-templates/overview) and [UTM Rules](/utm-rules/overview) - those two together prevent 80% of dirty attribution data.
</Note>
